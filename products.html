Voice Computing Research Group Products

Products

Qfrency

Our text-to-speech (TTS) system creates human-like voices from written text for the South African market.

These synthetic voices can say and read anything in South African languages, on a wide range of devices and platforms, for industry, developers and individuals.

For more information, please visit qfrency.com

iSinkwe

ISinkwe is an app that adds and synchronises human-narrated or computer-generated audio to text. The text can be written in any of the 11 official written languages of South Africa.

Publishers, schools and private individuals can use iSinkwe to create more accessible books, class notes and other documents, in order to open up the world of reading and learning to everyone. For more information, please isinkwe.com
